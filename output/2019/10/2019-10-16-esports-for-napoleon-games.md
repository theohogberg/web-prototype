##### **Esports are currently on our agenda and yesterday, on October 15th, we launched a brand new Esports Lobby for Napoleon Games.**

As an extension of the successful launch of [Napoleon Games Sportsbook 2.0](https://glomo.se/latest/napoleon-sportsbook/), a dedicated Esports Lobby has now been added to the Belgian market-leading iGaming provider’s offer.

The dev team is currently busy with trying out this new skin of Napoleon’s but will – as soon as the last hitting is over – level up this collaboration further.

Rest assured more projects of this kind are to come!
