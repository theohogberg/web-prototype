##### GLOMO has entered a collaboration with Gimi – an app that gives kids and teenagers Financial Superskills for Life​®​.

Gimi is a science-backed mobile app that allows today’s technologically savvy kids the ability to handle their own pocket money. With Gimi, users can earn, spend and save money through a playful and encouraging interface. Truly one of a kind, [Gimi provides a tool that teaches kids and teenagers financial literacy in the digital era.](http://www.gimitheapp.com)

The collaboration between Gimi and GLOMO includes native development and was initiated a few months ago. Gimi is currently in an expansive phase and we are very excited to follow them on their journey ahead!

The Gimi app is free of charge, but can be upgraded to a premium subscription, accompanied by the Gimi Card – a Mastercard® that furthers the mission of giving youths Financial Superskills for life®​.

Make sure you check out Gimi on ​[App Store​](https://apps.apple.com/app/gimi-a-piggy-bank/id935778197) or ​[Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=se.veckopengen.app)​.
