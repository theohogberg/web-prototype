GLOMO is proud to announce that we have started working with TVmatchen.nu (TVkampen.com in Norway and TVsporten.dk in Denmark). TVmatchen.nu delivers real-time sports TV listings, statistics and betting tips, to millions of sports fans across multiple markets.

TVmatchen.nu is available on web and mobile apps for both Android and iOS. GLOMO will primarily work on the TVmatchen websites and support new market expansions. The whole team at GLOMO are super excited to have TVmatchen.nu as one of our partners.
