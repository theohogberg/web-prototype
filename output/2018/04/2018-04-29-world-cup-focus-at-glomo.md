The GLOMO team have been working hard to deliver widgets for the World Cup 2018. The widgets are developed in close cooperation with Kambi where GLOMO have been a development partner since 2015. We have developed both customized operator specific widgets as well as, adding widgets to the Kambi core widget library. In addition to this we have created non-exclusive widgets that are possible to run for any Kambi customer. We are proud to present a wide range of World Cup widgets giving Kambi customers full potential to address the World Cup betting customers.
