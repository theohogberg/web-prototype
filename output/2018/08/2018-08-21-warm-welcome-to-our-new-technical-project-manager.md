We’re welcoming Maria Granlund to the GLOMO team. Maria will work as a Technical Project Manager to drive successful client projects together with the development team at GLOMO.

Most recently Maria worked as a Growth Manager at Proxify, where she focused on digitalisation and business expansion. Her previous experience will help GLOMO gain efficiencies in its daily processes and set up conditions for growth.

“I’m excited to join a team of top-notch developers and creative professionals working to deliver world-class projects for clients of many sizes and industries” says Maria.

Stephanie Kihlander, GLOMO COO says: “The recruitment of Maria further strengthen our efforts to improve efficiency and professionalism in delivering successful projects and solutions to our clients.”

You can connect with Maria on [LinkedIn](https://se.linkedin.com/in/maria-granlund-064a07103).

_About GLOMO: GLOMO is a company within Globalmouth Holding creating digitala and mobile solutions such as applications, web solutions and widgets for clients globally since 2002. GLOMO’s customers operate across a wide range of industries including sports betting, construction and public sector and includes big names like Kambi, Skanska, Mr Green, TV-matchen, Agria etc._
