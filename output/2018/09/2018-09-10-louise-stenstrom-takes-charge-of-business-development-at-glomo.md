Louise Stenström joined GLOMO before the summer as our new Head of Business Development, with the purpose to further develop our clients’ businesses and find new opportunities. She quickly settled into her role and is now up to speed with all clients, colleagues and our work here at GLOMO. We are very happy to have her on board.

With previous experience of working with digital communication of online services, Louise is a master of digital solutions and brings a new perspective and expertise to our business.

Christina Bäck, the CEO of GLOMO says: ‘With Louise’s strong drive, energy and customer focus she will be a key to the future success of GLOMO. She has been here for some months now and with a flying start she has already made a big difference for our business’’.

‘Overwhelmed by GLOMO’s amazing culture and the individuals that make this company, I simply couldn’t wish for better conditions as Head of Business Development. This team with the many creative, innovative, and highly engaged individuals is a joy to work in” says Louise.
